SurfPotDB

SurfPotDB is an open database of protein surface electrostatic potentials computed with a single, reproducible Poisson–Boltzmann protocol. For each covered PDB structure, SurfPotDB provides surface potential maps (in kT/e, T=298.15 K), electrostatic energy terms (in kT), protonated PQR files with per-atom charges and radii, and downloadable data for reuse in structural analysis and machine-learning workflows. Search by PDB code to retrieve the computed representative for that structure or, when applicable, the representative of its 90%-sequence-identity cluster.
